# MISSION

## Purpose

BroyhillGOP is an enterprise political operating system built to give North Carolina Republican candidates, committees, and party organizations a decisive data and technology advantage.

## Core Thesis

Every political dollar has an optimal target. Every voter contact has an optimal channel and moment. Every candidate has donors who are mathematically predisposed to support them. BroyhillGOP exists to find those optimal matches and execute on them at scale.

## Operational Scope

- **Geographic:** All 100 North Carolina counties, 13 congressional districts, 50 state senate districts, 120 state house districts, 550+ municipalities, 115 school boards
- **Data Universe:** 9 million registered NC voters, 7.6 million DataTrust-enriched profiles, 1.1 million FEC donation records, 652K state-level donation records
- **Ecosystem Count:** 58 functional ecosystems covering data import, identity resolution, donor intelligence, outreach channels, compliance, analytics, and AI/ML
- **Database:** 657 PostgreSQL tables in Supabase, 59 million total rows, 11,498 columns

## Design Principles

1. **Data-first** — Every decision flows from data, not intuition
2. **Identity-resolved** — One golden record per person across all data sources
3. **Multi-channel** — Coordinated outreach across mail, email, SMS, phone, digital, events, and social
4. **Candidate-portable** — Any candidate can activate their personalized donor file from the shared platform
5. **Compliance-native** — FEC and NCBOE rules are baked into the data model, not bolted on
